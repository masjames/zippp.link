Answers before you ask.

Do I need the WhatsApp Business API?

No. Zippp uses normal WhatsApp links. Your buyers tap and their WhatsApp opens with the order written. Nothing to install, no Meta approval, no setup call.

What does "order routing" actually mean?

If you have more than one number, each new order goes to the next number in turn. You can weight it so a busy person gets fewer, or send a returning buyer back to whoever they bought from last time.

How do buyers see my products?

You keep products and prices in a Google Sheet. Zippp turns that sheet into a page at zippp.link/yourshop. Edit the sheet and the page updates.

Does zippp take a cut of my sales?

No. Buyers pay you directly in WhatsApp. Zippp never touches the money, so you keep every dollar. No transaction fees, ever.

Is there a free plan?

A free plan for solo sellers with one number is coming. Routing across multiple numbers is the paid feature. Join the waitlist and you will hear when free opens.

When does it launch?

It is being built in public right now. The waitlist gets access first, and the first 100 shops get Pro free for a year. Join to hold your spot.

Who is behind this?
