Frequently asked questions

Do I need Shopify?
No. Zippp works standalone. If you have Shopify, paste your store URL like myshopify.com/products.json, or upload products_export.csv, to import in 2 clicks.
How do I collect payment?
Buyers send a pre-filled WhatsApp message with their order and total. You reply with your bank details or wallet app directly in the chat.

We use Polar.sh as Merchant of Record. Works from Indonesia, India, Nigeria, Brazil, and the Philippines. Handles VAT, pays out to Wise. No LLC needed. 4% + 40¢ fee.
