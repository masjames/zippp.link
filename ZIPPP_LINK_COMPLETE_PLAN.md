# PART A — PRODUCT OVERVIEW & POSITIONING

## The Product

zippp.link is a WhatsApp-first sales page builder for small sellers. A seller adds products (photo, name, price, description) and each product card has a "Chat on WhatsApp" button with a pre-filled message. The buyer browses products on a web page, taps a product, and starts a WhatsApp conversation ready to order.

## Killer Positioning Statement

> **"Linktree starts links. zippp.link starts sales."**

## Target User

Small sellers in Indonesia (and Global South) who close orders manually in WhatsApp:
- Home kitchen sellers (makanan rumahan)
- Home bakers & cake makers
- Coffee stalls & small cafes
- Fashion boutiques & hijab sellers

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | What You Get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Free** | $0 | 1 page, 6 products, basic QR sharing, zippp.link branding |
| **Pro Yearly** | $12/year ($1/mo) | 3 pages, 100 products, branded QR, analytics, FAQ/hours/location blocks, remove branding |
| **Pro Lifetime** | $49 one-time | Same as Pro Yearly, pay once keep forever |

**DO NOT raise prices.** $1/mo is a strategic moat. No VC-funded competitor can match it profitably.

## The 3 Gaps We Own (No Competitor Has These)

1. **"The WhatsApp Sales Page"** — No competitor offers a beautiful, shareable web page that displays YOUR products AND initiates WhatsApp chats with pre-filled messages. Linktree shows links. WhatsApp Business catalog is trapped inside the app. Shorby has WhatsApp buttons but zero product display.

2. **"The $1/Month Micro-Seller"** — The cheapest product-selling competitor is Beacons at $10/mo or Stan Store at $29/mo. zippp.link is 4-25x cheaper. This owns the micro-seller segment entirely.

3. **"Radical Simplicity"** — Purposefully simple: product cards, photos, prices, WhatsApp chat button. No checkout, no payment processing, no email capture, no courses. Just: see product → chat on WhatsApp → close manually.

## Indonesian Market Context

- 64M+ MSMEs in Indonesia, only 22M (34%) have gone digital
- 87-112M WhatsApp users in Indonesia
- 73.1M WhatsApp Business app downloads (2nd globally)
- 64.5% of MSMEs are women-led (~37M businesses)
- Conversational commerce: $12.68B in 2023, projected $30.25B by 2028

1. **Zero visual hierarchy** — everything has equal weight

# PART D — PRODUCT FLOW & UX SPEC

## Optimized User Flow

```
LANDING PAGE (with embedded LIVE demo)
    |
    v
TEMPLATE PICKER (4 templates with live preview)
    |
    v
EDITOR (split screen: edit LEFT, preview RIGHT)
    |    <- Pre-filled example products
    |    <- Real-time preview updates
    |
    v
PUBLISH (one tap)
    |
    v
SHARE SHEET (link, WhatsApp, Instagram, QR)
    |
    v
TRIGGER-BASED UPGRADE PROMPTS
    |
    v
PRO UPGRADE ($12/year or $49 lifetime)
```

## Flow Rules

### 1. Landing Page
- Show a LIVE embedded demo (actual working coffee stall page, not a screenshot)
- Counter: "X,XXX sellers already selling" — live user count
- 3-step visual: Add Products → Share Link → Get Orders on WhatsApp
- CTA: "Buat Halaman Gratis" (not "Sign Up" — action language)
- Trust bar: "Free forever. No credit card. 30-second setup."

### 2. Signup
- **CRITICAL FIX:** Allow page creation WITHOUT signup. Let users build first, collect email at publish time.
- Every extra step loses ~50% of users.
- Use a managed auth provider only after the static launch proves demand; the current Vercel launch keeps setup friction at zero.

### 3. Onboarding — Template Picker
- Screen immediately after signup shows 4 templates as cards
- Each template has: thumbnail preview + name + 1-line description
- One-question onboarding: "What do you sell?" → auto-suggests template
- Templates: DapurKit (Food), OvenCraft (Bakery), KopiSpot (Coffee), StyleLink (Fashion)

### 4. Editor — Split Screen
- **LEFT:** Edit form (product name, price, photo upload, description)
- **RIGHT:** Live preview updating in real-time
- Pre-populated with 3-4 example products for chosen template
- Inline tooltips: "Tap to edit this product" / "Upload your photo here"

### 5. Publish
- One-tap publish button
- Immediate share options:
  - Copy Link
  - Share to WhatsApp
  - Share to Instagram
  - Download QR Code
- Confetti animation on first publish

### 6. Post-Publish
- Auto-generated Instagram Story image: "I just launched my menu!"
- Pre-written WhatsApp broadcast: "Hi! I've just created my digital menu. See all my products here: [link]"

### 7. Upgrade Triggers (Free → Pro)
- At 5/6 products: "Add unlimited products with Pro — only Rp190K/year"
- When trying to remove branding: "Remove zippp.link logo with Pro"
- When downloading QR: "Get branded QR codes with Pro"
- After 50 page views: "See who's viewing with Pro Analytics"
- After 7 days: Email sequence with Founding Member scarcity

### 8. Payment
- **Primary:** Xendit or Midtrans (Indonesian payment — supports QRIS, bank transfer)
- **International:** PayPal
- **Fallback:** Manual bank transfer + WhatsApp confirmation
- **CRITICAL:** Must be implemented before Month-1 launch. Without payment, $2K is impossible.
